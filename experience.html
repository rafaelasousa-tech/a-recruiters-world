the beginning.

the goal.

In Lisbon, I graduated Psychology from ISCTE in 2016.

I entered the recruitment world, the same year I graduated, via an internship. Those 3 months opened the door to a new world: start-ups.

Before I finished my internship, I got my first full-time job in a technology start-up. There I learned the foundations of recruitment and started to navigate the waters of tech. However, as I got more interested in tech, I decided to look for a new chapter as I wanted to be closer to the engineering teams. I got that opportunity in another amazing start-up, where alone I was supporting the hiring of 15 teams.

This 15 teams were all I asked for in terms of learning. But life has funny ways to challenge you.

An invite to start a start-up from the ground up came my way, and the chance to build everything recruitment and people from zero.

How could I say no? 10 months later, we moved offices twice, and alone I scaled the company from 2 (me and the CEO) to over 30 people, while recruitment, sourcing and relocation frameworks were established.

This was going very well but at some point recruitment started to slow down and I wanted to continue to learn.

I always wanted to pursue a career abroad and join a company with a common goal. A company that builds a product(s) to help others. Besides, as I mostly worked alone, I was really hoping to be in a team.

So I started to look around. Things went well and after 1 year, here I am! Living in the Silicon Valley of Europe for one of the fastest growing fintech companies in Europe.
